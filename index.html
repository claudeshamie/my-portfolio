    About

    About Us

    At Jerks Auto Mechanics we have dedicated staff of highly skilled and experienced mechanics, that maintain a high ethical standard, to assure you, the client, that you are dealing with a reputable vehicle workshop, that prides itself on high quality repairs/service to your vehicle. We have access to, and maintain, up to date diagnostic vehicle data, and therefore no work will be carried out on your vehicle without your prior knowledge or consent.

    Customer service and care are our number one priority and remain the core of our business thus we strive to

    • Build lasting relationships with our customers in the process
    • Remain a highly efficient, technologically advanced and forward moving company to meet the needs of our customers
    • Empower our employees to be the best they can be
